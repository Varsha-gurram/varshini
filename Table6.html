Chemical Reaction Timeline

  1. Reaction:Combustion of Hydrogen
    2H2+O2→2H2O
    Explanation:This reaction releases energy as water (2H2O)is formed. It is an exothermic reaction, meaning it releases heat.
  2. Reaction:Dissociation od water
    2H2O→H++OH-
    Explanation:Water dissociates into Hydrogen ions(H+) and (OH-). This process is essential in acid-base chemistry.
  3. Reaction:Synthesis of Ammonia(Haber Process)
    N2+3H2→NH3(Incorrect Stoichiometry)
    Corrected:N2+3H2→2NH3
    Explanation:This reaction combines nitrogen(N2) and hydrogen(H2)to form Ammonia(NH3).It requires a high temperature and pressure.
